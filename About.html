About Us

Built on Banking Experience and Customer Trust

Established on 09 May 2025, we bring together industry experts with strong experience in retail lending and credit services.

With a deep understanding of banking processes and customer needs, we work closely with reputed banks and NBFCs to provide seamless loan solutions. Our goal is to simplify the borrowing journey for individuals and businesses through transparent guidance and efficient service.

We are committed to ethical practices, prompt customer support, and delivering the right financial solutions tailored to each client's requirements.

What We Do

End-to-End Loan Lifecycle Support

At Loan Mart, we support customers, banks, and NBFC partners across every stage of the loan journey.

Application Sourcing

Sourcing and processing of loan applications including home loans, personal loans, auto loans, business loans, and more.

Onboarding & KYC

Customer onboarding, document verification, and KYC compliance handled with clarity and care.

Bank Coordination

Coordination between customers and banks for approvals, documentation, and disbursement.

Post-Loan Support

Support for EMI queries, top-up loans, balance transfer, customer issue resolution, and collections assistance.
